# V1 Epics

Document ID: VS-EPIC-V1

Version: 0.1

Status: Draft

Last updated: 2026-02-05

## EPIC-1 Member Booking Experience

Goal: Make it effortless for members to find and book classes.

Includes:
- schedule browsing + filters
- booking and cancellation rules
- waitlist join + promotion

## EPIC-2 Staff Scheduling Operations

Goal: Provide staff with clear control over the schedule and capacity.

Includes:
- class type management
- one-off class creation/edit
- capacity visibility and waitlist management

## EPIC-3 Instructor Management

Goal: Make instructor scheduling operationally safe and auditable.

Includes:
- instructor profiles
- availability and time off
- conflict prevention and admin override
- instructor swap workflow

## EPIC-4 Auditability and Governance

Goal: Ensure critical actions are traceable.

Includes:
- audit events for key operational actions
- audit log viewer for staff/admin

## EPIC-5 Quality Gates

Goal: Maintain a predictable release pipeline.

Includes:
- build/lint/typecheck gates
- documented test plan and scripts
