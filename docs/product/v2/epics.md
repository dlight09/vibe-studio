# V2 Epics

Document ID: VS-EPIC-V2

Version: 0.1

Status: Draft

Last updated: 2026-02-05

## EPIC-1 Membership Entitlements

Goal: Ensure bookings are gated by unlimited subscriptions or credits.

Includes:
- entitlement evaluation rules
- booking enforcement and feedback
- credit consumption/refund

## EPIC-2 Counter Sales Operations

Goal: Enable staff to sell plans and record payments without online checkout.

Includes:
- counter sale flow
- payment capture metadata
- member entitlement updates

## EPIC-3 Plan Lifecycle Management

Goal: Allow admins to create, activate, and deactivate plans.

Includes:
- plan CRUD (create/activate/deactivate)
- visibility of plan status

## EPIC-4 Auditability for Memberships

Goal: Provide traceability for membership and payment actions.

Includes:
- audit events for plans, purchases, payments, credits
- visibility in admin audit viewer

## EPIC-5 Quality Gates

Goal: Maintain a predictable release pipeline.

Includes:
- build/typecheck gates
- documented V2 test plan and scripts
